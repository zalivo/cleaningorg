# cleaningorg

Welcome to **CleaningOrg** — your go-to platform for professional cleaning services.

## About

CleaningOrg connects customers with trusted cleaning professionals for residential and commercial spaces. Whether you need a one-time deep clean or recurring maintenance, we've got you covered.

## Features

- Book cleaning appointments online
- Browse verified cleaning professionals
- Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Secure payments and invoicing
- Real-time booking status updates

## Getting Started

1. Create an account
2. Enter your location and service needs
3. Choose a cleaning professional
4. Confirm your booking and relax
